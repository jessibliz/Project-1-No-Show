Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

This Dataset contains information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil. The Dataset contains a total of 110526 rows and 14 columns with headings as follows: PatientId: identifies the Identity number of each Patient. AppointmentID: shows the appointment number of each Patient. Gender: classifies Patients sex as either Male or a Female. ScheduledDay: shows the Date of appointment and Time AppointmentDay: shows only the Date of Appointment Age: gives information about the Age of individual Patient. Neigbourhood: indicates the location of the Facilility Scholarship: shows Patient under any form of Scholarship programm Hipertension: shows Patient having Hipertension. Diabetes: identifies Patient suffering from Diabetes. Alcoholism: shows Patient who are Alcoholic Handcap: shows having Physical chanlleges. SMS_received: gives information about Patient that received SMS or not No-show: indicates Patient that showed up for their appointment.

Question(s) for Analysis¶

Could factors such as Scholarship,Age,SMS_received and Hipertension affect why Patient didn't show up for their Scheduled-Appountment?

Conclusions¶

In the first part I analysed the non attendance of Patients to Scheduled appointment.I made my analysation based on the statics of Patients that failed to meet up with their appointment and the Age.I found a negative correlations as the plot skewed to the left.

I further plotted two independent variable(SMS_received' and 'Scholarship) against the dependent variable(No_show), 'SMS_received' and 'Scholarship' which showed higher level of correlations with 'No_show' data recorded in the dataframe.

Limitations
